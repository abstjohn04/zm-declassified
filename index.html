Purpose

Have you ever wanted to look up the steps to an easter-egg or wonder-weapon quest for a zombies map you are currently playing only to find a website with pop-up ads, and several hour-long videos? Me too...

That's exactly why I made this site... ("made" is a strong word)

This website is meant to be an easy-to-read, pop-up free, fast-loading guide to every Call of Duty Zombies that won't make looking up steps during a match impossible.

Issues

I am not a zombies influencer. I do not make in-depth map guides. I am trying my best, and I am guaranteed to miss things and get some things wrong.

If you find an issue, please submit it as an issue in the Github repository. I want to make this website as useful as possible for myself and others.

Final remarks

The layout of this site was influenced (if not just completely stolen) from Fabien Sanglard's Website. The guy has some really cool articles about his experience as a software engineer. Please check him out — especially if you are into software graphics renderers.
