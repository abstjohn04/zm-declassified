Wonder Weapon: Wrath of the Ancients & Upgrades

The wonder weapon on this map is a set of elemental bows (and a base bow). Once the base version (Wrath of the Ancients) is retrieved, it can be updated to one of four variants.

Wrath of the Ancients

There are several dragon statues around the map that will come to life when a zombie is killed by them. Feed them zombies to unlock the bow.

    Dragon locations:
  • Lower Courtyard
  • Church
  • Undercroft

Once filled, the bow will spawn on the pedestal in the tomb room.

The Lightning Bow
Arrow (Start Upgrade Quest)

Shoot the weathervane on top of the building on the death ray platform with the Wrath of the Ancients, and the arrow will fall.

Light the Bonfires

There are three bonfires around the map that you need to light with the Wrath of the ancients.

    Bonfire locations:
  • Past KRM wallbuy near Double Tap
  • Behind Clocktower
  • On hill outside Rocket Test Site
You can actually hit the Rocket Test Site bonfire without leaving the main area. Just go to the room above Double Tap and shoot out of the window, aiming here.
Wall Run

In the Undercroft, during the anti-gravity phase, run across all the glowing symbols on the wall.

Electrify The Bonfires

There will now be a sparking urn near the bonfire locations. Fill it with a few zombie souls. Now if you draw your bow near it, the bow will begin sparking. Hit each of the bonfires once more with a sparking arrow.

You will actually need to go to Rocket Test this time.
Retrieve Mended Arrow

Interacting with the place you retrieved the arrow will cause an animation of the arrow being re-constructed. Grab it and place it in the corresponding pedestal in the Undercroft. Once you kill enough zombies and see their souls enter the arrow (~25) you will be able to interact and retrieve the lightning bow.

The Wolf Bow
Arrow (Start Upgrade Quest)

To retrieve the arrow, you must activate four different paintings around the map in the correct order.

    The correct painting order:
  1. Wolf King on his throne
  2. Wolf King on his horse
  3. The battle scene
  4. Wolf King death
    The paintings spawn randomly in four different locations around the map:
  • Hallway from bedrooms to power room
  • First floor of the Clocktower
  • Below the Clocktower
  • Second story of church building - right side

Once all the paintings have been interacted with in the correct order, the arrow will burst out of the wall in the Undercroft near the stairs to the Wrath of the Ancients.

Retrieve Wolf Skull

With the arrow, travel to Rocket Test Site, and locate a flag planted on the mountain side, above the enterence to the tunnel. Shoot the flag with the Wrath of the Ancients.

Once the flag has been shot, a wolf skull will fall from the mountain onto the ground next to a zombie barricade. Interact to pick it up.

Walk the Wolf

With the skull, travel to the Undercroft and locate the Wolf Bow pedestal in the center. There will be a wolf skeleton missing its skull. Interact to place the skull you picked up.

Once you interact, a wolf will spawn, and you will need to follow it around. Occasionally, the wolf will stop and dig. In that spot, you will need to get zombie kills until the wolf digs once more.

The wolf will dig up a bone that needs to be interacted with to pick up before the wolf will continue.

After three locations, the wolf will be finished and run to a spot on the wall in the Undercroft across from Pack-a-Punch.

Retrieve Mended Arrow

During anti-gravity, jump and wallrun to see that a wolf symbol begins glowing on the wall above where the wolf is sitting. Once shot, a platform will be built underneath the symbol.

Stand on the platform and interact with the hole in the wall to place the bones. The newly assembled skeleton will hand you the mended arrow. Grab it and place it in the corresponding pedestal in the Undercroft. Once you kill enough zombies and see their souls enter the arrow (~25) you will be able to interact and retrieve the wolf bow.

The Void Bow
Arrow (Start Upgrade Quest)

Shoot the ceiling marked with purple symbols in the gatehouse with Double Tap to retrieve the broken void bow arrow.

Activate Urn

Once the quest is started, go underneath the Clocktower and see a square tile on the ground glowing purple. Get a knife kill with the zombie overtop this tile to break it.

Underneath the tile, you will see an urn. Interact with it to raise it in the air.

Retrieve Skulls
    You must now collect six skulls from around the map:
  • In broken wall on staircase leading to Mule Kick room
  • On Church windowsill on path behind well in Main Courtyard
  • In sink basin in teleporter room
  • On broken wall segment next to Double Tap
  • In toybox in Samantha's bedroom
  • In the back of a truck at Rocket Test Site
Feed the Skulls

The skulls have teleported to be around the urn, and you must bring zombie crawlers into the room for each skull to eath (6 total).

Enter His Name
    Now, the urn will say three of six possible words that correspond to statues found in the hallway outside of the power room:
  • Heart
  • Stag
  • Door
  • Crown
  • Griffin
  • Horn

Interacting with these statues will unveil Apothicon symbols. The Apothicon symbols in the corresponding order is his name.

You must now kill zombies. Every few zombies will drop one of the Apothicon symbols, and it will appear on the floor around the urn. Once you have all the symbols, you can shoot the symbols in the order of his name.

If you do this incorrectly, the skulls will start to attack you. Just leave and try again later.
Retrieve Mended Arrow

Once his name has been entered, a purple rift will form. Interacting with it will craft the mended arrow. Grab it and place it in the corresponding pedestal in the Undercroft. Once you kill enough zombies and see their souls enter the arrow (~25) you will be able to interact and retrieve the void bow.

The Fire Bow
This bow can be pretty difficult with many steps requiring precise timing. Make sure the player doing this upgrade quest has decent ping.
Arrow (Start Upgrade Quest)

At the top of the Clocktower, behind the clock, there is a fire symbol. Shoot it to reveal the broken fire arrow.

Shoot Volcano

At Rocket Test Site, there will be a volcano on the jut-out next to the wundersphere pad. Immediately after a rocket test sequence, the tip of the volcano will glow. You need to shoot the tip while it is glowing.

This step is really tough and requires your shot to be almost perfect. It might take more than one try.
Wundersphere step
    Three cuniform rings have appeared on the map:
  • On the ramp leading to Double Tap
  • On platform outside of Clocktower
  • On Death Ray platform

You need to hit these rings with a Wrath of the Ancients while flying through the air in the Wundersphere. Once hit, the ring will remain lit and act as a soulbox. Stand in the ring and get kills.

Light the fireplace

Back where you got the arrow, behind the clock, interact with the gears of the clock and a cuniform symbol will be revealed. This symbol matches one of three fireplaces around the map.

    Fireplaces:
  • In Maxis' bedroom
  • Room above Lower Courtyard with Wunderfizz
  • In the Church

One of the rings will be glowing. You need to stand in the ring and shoot towards the correct fireplace. When it lands, a volcano will appear, and you can shoot another volcano. You have 4 total shots to reach the fireplace.

Retrieve Mended Arrow

Once lit, the ball of fire at Death Ray can be interacted with. It will explode and drop the reforged arrow. Grab it and place it in the corresponding pedestal in the Undercroft. Once you kill enough zombies and see their souls enter the arrow (~25) you will be able to interact and retrieve the fire bow.

Ragnorok DG-4
Part #1

Retrieved by killing the Panzersoldat for the first time. Will drop after next Panzersoldat kill if not picked up.

Part #2

After activating Death Ray, will be floating in the path of one of the wunderspheres. Take the proper wundersphere to pick it up.

Part #3

While the rocket test sequence is beginning, flip the switch found near the platform steps by PaP. Quickly run into the building. Once the test is over, you will be able to interact with a switch on the control panel next to the teleporter. Once flipped, the piece will spawn in the teleporter.

Main Easter Egg: My Brother's Keeper
Step 1: Upgrade a bow

It doesn't matter which bow, but to start the egg you need to have upgraded the Wrath of the Ancients into one of its four upgraded versions.

Step 2: Whisp step (time travel)

Take the Wrath of the Ancients to the teleporter room in the Undercroft. Here you will shoot the glowing orbs connected to the top of the teleporter. Once all have been shot, you should hear an activation shound (Margwa growl).

Whisps of light will appear around the map that must be shot an upgraded variant of the bow. The whisp will respawn four times. When completed, you should hear a different activations sound. If you fail, you'll hear a loud buzz.

Do not use the Wrath of the Ancients to shoot the whisps. This will auto-fail the step.
    Whisp spawn locations:
  • Typewriter near Quick Revive
  • Tire on Double Tap slope
  • In crate above Double Tap (Left of window)
  • Globe in the bedrooms
  • Phone on pillar in power room
  • Clock in Barracks
  • Radio antenna in Chuch
  • Clock above fireplace in Church
Time Travel

Once completed, the teleporter should be glowing purple. All players need to take it to be teleported back in time.

    In the past, immediately grab these items:
  • Blue Canister
  • Fuses on crate

Now, just make sure you remember the code that Dr. Groph puts into the safe (IMPORTANT).

Input safe code

You will spawn at Rocket Test and so will a Panzersoldat. Run past him or kill him, but don't stick around because the test will be beginning.

Upon returning to the castle, make sure you have used the Death Ray once during your game.

It's odd, but without using the Death Ray, you cannot enter the code.

At the console outside of Clocktower, the smaller four terminals will be showing symbols. Enter the safe code and you should hear the success sound from earlier.

If you run to the teleporter room, you will see that the safe is open, and you can grab the items within.

Step 3: Death ray step (bring down Dempsey)

You can place the tubes you picked up in the sides of the death ray beams.

Then, place the fuses in the side of the Death Ray, and make sure the dial is set to destroy.

If the Death Ray is not set to destroy, you will not be able to arm it, but the game will still let you do the steps (though nothing will happen).

Activating the terminal from before, you will need to remember the symbols on the four monitors and play a game of memory. Just activate the terminal based on the symbol shown on the big screen. This should be done on both the console from before and the console at Rocket Test Site.

Once armed, the Death Ray balls will be glowing white.

Activate the now glowing green button on the back of the Death Ray to zap Dempsey out of the sky.

Step 4: Fill the Keeper stones

After Dempsey crashes, the Vril Generator can be found in the center of the Main Courtyard. Pick it up.

You can take the Vril Generator to the tomb where you retrieved the Wrath of the Ancients to spawn in a Keeper. But first, you need to go back in time to retrieve something else. Just do the Whisp step exactly like before, and when you teleport back you will be able to place a disc you retrieved from the safe into one of the computers. This will open a chest by the door, revealing a keeper stone which you must pick up.

You can actually do this as soon as you open the safe. The whisps will continue to spawn at the start of every round until you do this.

You can now spawn the Keeper and follow him to four different locations, filling his stones with the proper bow. The ring he creates on the floor will give you a glowing vignette that tells which elemental upgrade is required.

Unlike similar maps, you don't have to have all four bows. You only need one bow for each player. However, if you start an upgrade quest, that bow will be required for this step.
There is a well-known bug where a Panzersoldat spawning on top of the Keeper while he is moving can cause the Keeper to be stuck forever. Just be careful.

One location will not have a stone on the wall (near Double Tap), which is convenient because you just retrieved one from the past. Once the final stone is full, the Keeper will materialize. Follow him to the Undercroft.

Step 5: Enter Boss Fight

After some cool dialog, the MPD will spawn where the stone pyramid once was. You need to place the blue canister (from step 2) in the corner without a canister to open it.

You should have the Ragnorok DG-4 by now. All players must place their Ragnoroks while standing on the blue squares surrounding the MPD simultaneously. This will begin the boss fight.

Keeper Boss Fight
Step 6: The Moon

Once the Keeper is defeated, you need to activate the Summoning Key on the front panel of the MPD until you pick it up.

Now just take the Summoning Key to the console near Clocktower and interact with it. Congratulations! You did it!

Miscellaneous Easter Eggs
Skeleton Zombies

Shooting three floating skulls around the map will turn zombies into skeletons.

You can only actually see these skulls with the In Plain Sight gobblegum. However, you can still do it without seeing them.
  • Ledge near Mule Kick
  • In front of the stained glass, above the main Church door
  • Ledge near Double Tap

The skulls will appear on a chair in the Church. Interact with them to turn the zombies back to zombies.

Plunger

Place the Ragnorok DG-4 behind the clock in the Clocktower.

Then interact with a switch inside the Clocktower to make it turn. Once the clock hits 9:35, you must hit the same switch to stop it.

Now, wall run over the blue circle near the entrance to the Undercroft. You will be sent back in time.

In the past, pick up the plunger next to the desk.

Free Mega Gobblegum

Pick up the plant vase in the teleporter room.

Once in the past (see Main Easter-Egg guide), place the plant back.

Back in the present, the plant will be grown, and interacting with it will give you a free, random mega gobblegum.
