Der Riese ‐ World at War
Main Easter Egg Solo Friendly
Main Easter Egg: Fly Trap
Step 1: Pack-a-Punch

The first step of the easter-egg requires you to open the Pack-a-Punch (like you would have done already). Just link all three teleporters and pack-a-punch one of your weapons.

Make sure to PaP a weapon that has a decently long range. Just not a shotgun.
Step 2: Shoot the panel

With your PaP'ed weapon, run to the area to the right of the Pack-a-Punch. Past the zombie barrier, you'll see a control panel in the distance.

Shoot that with your newly PaP'ed gun. You'll know you hit it if you see green objects floating from the area. You've started the easter-egg.

Step 3: Shoot the items

Three items have spawned across the map. You need to shoot them.

Teddy Bear #1: Animal Testing Lab

The first teddy bear can be found in an animal crate on the floor in the room behind the barrier where you shot the panel.

Teddy Bear #2: Spawn

The second teddy bear is in a window that you can see from spawn.

Monkey Bomb: Furnace Room

The monkey bomb is in the furnace located in the room under Teleporter B.

Musical Easter Egg: Beauty of Annihilation

To activate the song, you just need to interact with three green jars around the map.

Green Jar #1: Animal Testing Lab

On a desk next to the staircase debris door.

Green Jar #2: Animal Testing Lab

On the shelf opposite the desk.

Green Jar #3: Teleporter B

On a desk in the side room by Teleporter B.
