Main Easter Egg: Ensemble Cast
Step 1: Fuse

After turning on power, make your way to the room under the Lighthouse Station. Here there will be a powered door with the Ultimis crew behind it. Knife it and listen to the dialog.

    You need to find the fuse for the crew in the room above the door:
  • Table next to Phd Flopper
  • Desk next to staircase
  • Leaning against a locker

Take this fuse and place it in the panel next to the door. Knife the door to hear more dialog.

Step 2: Destroy Generators

There will be several generators around outside of the map. You need to destroy them using some explosive weapon/grenade. Once destoryed, their red light will turn off.

    Generator locations:
  • Off of the path between the ship Stern and Forecastle
  • In the water, on the port side of the ship's Stern
  • Outside of a zombie barrier in the boathouse (building with Stamin-up)
  • In the room with Ultimis, across from the powered door
If you are having trouble, try using semtex and make sure it sticks to the top of the generator (where the light is).

Remember to knife the door to hear the next lines of dialog.

Step 3: Nikolai's vodka
    A bottle of vodka has spawned somewhere on the map encased in ice:
  • On railing above door to PhD Flopper
  • On catwalk outside of door to PhD Flopper
  • On railing near the M16 wallbuy on the ship
  • Catwalk above the path between the ship halves

One player needs to knife the bottle, while the other stands underneath and catches it. The player with the bottle then needs to place it in the pneumatic tube next to the powered door to deliver it to Nikolai.

Remember to knife the door to hear the next lines of dialog.

Step 4: Morse Code Radios
    There are four radios that need to be activated in order:
  1. On top of a cabinet in the room underneath power
  2. On barrel across from Stamin-up
  3. Forecastle, inside of a shipping container near door
  4. On cabinet in the same room as the powered door

Remember to knife the door to hear the next lines of dialog.

Step 5: Signal the Submarine

Go to the power room. There are three levers and a wheel.

Set the wheel so that the brown handle is facing 5 o'clock.

Set the left handle to 1 click, and set the right handle to 3 clicks.

You will hear a foghorn, and a submarine will surface in the water off the starboard side of the forecastle.

Step 6: Activate foghorns
    There are four foghorns that must be activated in order:
  1. Base of Lighthouse, near pool of water
  2. To the left of door to Speed Cola
  3. Base of Lighthouse, next to wooden path
  4. Outside of room with Speed Cola, next to large boulder
Step 7: Dial Step
    There are four dials, one on each floor of the Lighthouse:
  • Yellow: floor 4
  • Orange: floor 3
  • Blue: floor 2
  • Purple: floor 1
    Set the dials in this order:
  1. Yellow: 2
  2. Blue: until Orange is 7
  3. Purple: 6
  4. Orange: until Blue is 4
  5. Yellow: 2
    After this, the final order should be:
  • Yellow: 2
  • Orange: 7
  • Blue: 4
  • Purple: 6

Remember to knife the door to hear the next lines of dialog.

Step 8: V-R11

Use the V-R11 to turn a zombie near the Lighthouse. It will begin to float in the center.

Before he reaches the top, kill him. He will drop the Golden Rod.

Now, just take the Golden Rod to the door and knife the fuse box.

Main Easter Egg (solo): Stand-in

The solo version of the easter egg is condensed to allow a user to complete it without another player.

    The steps are the same; however, the following steps can be skipped:
  • Step 3: Nikolai's vodka
  • Step 4: Morse Code Radios
  • Step 6: Activate foghorns
  • Step 7: Dial Step
Musical Easter Egg: Not Ready to Die
    Activate three meteor fragments around the map:
  • Spawn room, across from box location
  • Ship Kitchen, in a booth
  • Under a table in the PhD Flopper room
